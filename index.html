Tommy Marshall

Tommy has been a Front-end Developer at Viget for over two and a half years. While at Viget he has worked with clients such as PUMA, Dick's Sporting Goods, and OPower, and on projects like ESPN's Anatomy of a Pitch and Viget's own SayViget.com. Outside of coding he enjoys home brewing, playing guitar poorly, and getting the most out of his Amazon prime account.

Meetups consist of a twenty minute presentation followed by forty minutes of Q&A and hacking. All skill levels are welcome and encouraged to attend.
