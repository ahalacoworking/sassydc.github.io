Upcoming

Intro to Mapbox Studio

(register)

October 22, 2014 at 6:30pm
Canvas Coworking Space
1203 19th St. NW, 3rd Floor
Washington, DC 20036

Learn how to design a beautiful custom map for your next website or app! We'll walk through the whole process – from data to live map – using Mapbox Studio, a new map design tool. We'll also take a closer look at styling in Mapbox Studio with CartoCSS, a language based on CSS.

Nicki Dlugash

Nicki is a designer and cartographer at Mapbox, where she splits her time between making maps and making tools to help others make maps.

Meetups consist of a twenty minute presentation followed by forty minutes of Q&A and hacking. All skill levels are welcome and encouraged to attend.
