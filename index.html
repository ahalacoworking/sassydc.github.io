Upcoming

Intro to Sass Workshop!

(register)

April 16, 2015 at 6:30pm
Canvas Coworking Space
1203 19th St. NW, 3rd Floor
Washington, DC 20036

Have you been wanting to learn Sass? Now is your chance! Join us on Thursday, April 16th to learn how to improve your CSS workflow with Sass. We'll cover why Sass is awesome, how to install, how to transition from CSS to Sass, and go over some of the cool things you can do with Sass.

This workshop is geared towards those who understand the basics of CSS. If you're already fluent in Sass and want to help out, please come!
